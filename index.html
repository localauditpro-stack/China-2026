LOGISTICS & COMFORT
🚄

Transport

  • Primary: High-Speed Rail (HSR) network
  • HSR: Shanghai → Chengdu (bullet train, ~11hrs)
  • HSR: Chengdu → Chongqing (30 mins — world's fastest intercity)
  • HSR/Flight: Chongqing → Zhangjiajie (~5hrs)
  • HSR: Zhangjiajie → Guilin → Yangshuo (~4hrs)
  • HSR/Flight: Yangshuo → Hong Kong (~3hrs)
  • Chengdu–Chongqing is the world's busiest HSR corridor
💳

Payments

  • 100% digital — Alipay & WeChat Pay
  • Linked to Australian bank cards
  • Alipay search: 宝可梦卡牌 = Pokémon Cards
🌐

Navigation

  • Baidu Translate for communication
  • Amap for navigation (better than Google in CN)
  • VPN recommended for Google / Instagram

THE RADAR
🚁 Drone Regs

CAAC registration required >250g. No-fly: Beijing / Shanghai Bund. More freedom in Zhangjiajie & Yangshuo.

🗣 Language

Zero Mandarin. Rely on Baidu Translate + Amap. Younger locals in major cities understand basic English.

🎴 Pokémon TCG

Focus on Simplified Chinese (CS) Exclusive Sets. Shanghai & Chongqing official gyms. Huaqiangbei for imports.

🏋️ Fitness

High verticality in Zhangjiajie & Chongqing. Leg-day heavy. Frequent massage recovery strongly recommended.
