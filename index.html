THE RADAR
🚁 Drone Regs

CAAC registration required >250g. No-fly: Beijing / Shanghai Bund. More freedom in Zhangjiajie & Yangshuo.

🗣 Language

Zero Mandarin. Rely on Baidu Translate + Amap. Younger locals in major cities understand basic English.

🎴 Pokémon TCG

Focus on Simplified Chinese (CS) Exclusive Sets. Shanghai & Chongqing official gyms. Huaqiangbei for imports.

🏋️ Fitness

High verticality in Zhangjiajie & Chongqing. Leg-day heavy. Frequent massage recovery strongly recommended.
