🔍 FAKE-SPOTTING — CHINA MARKET RULES
Safe by default: official gyms, the Tmall/Taobao 宝可梦官方旗舰店, and mall chain stores. Verify at indie stores: sealed product should have factory shrink with the official anti-counterfeit label — scratch/QR verification codes on legit SC boxes; scan before paying if the seller allows. Singles: check holo pattern under phone torch (fakes have flat/uniform shine), card back saturation (fakes skew light blue), and font weight. Price sanity: check Jihuanshe (集换社 app) market price on the spot — anything 30%+ below market without a reason IS the reason. Never buy "graded" slabs from street stalls or night markets.

BEIJING 北京
DAYS 1–3 · AUG 21–23 · 3 NIGHTS
🏨 ~A$390–450🚇 L1L2L4L8L10
Imperial heavyweight, the trip's best photography, and your first gym promos. You land jet-lagged — pace it.
Top 3 Must-Dos
1. Great Wall — Jinshanling. Walk Main Gate → East Gate, 7–8 km, 31 towers, 3.5–4 hrs. Wild, unrestored, near-empty. ¥65.
2. Forbidden City. ¥60, enter Meridian Gate, exit north. Jingshan Park for the rooftop hero shot.
3. Temple of Heaven, 6:30am. Taichi, sword dancing, jianzi circles. The grannies will wave you in.
🎴 Gym & 🍸 Nightlife
Larvitar 幼基拉斯 — Zhongguancun Link Plaza B1, Line 4. Day 2, 8:30pm — COMMITTED. Optional 2nd: Onix at LIVAT Xihongmen.
Gulou hutong crawl. El Nido dive (beers from the fridge, benches in the alley) · Paddy O'Shea's FREE QUIZ Wed 8pm · Dada for techno.
⚠ WATCH Forbidden City tickets: 7-day window, drop 20:00 Beijing (10pm Melb) — for these dates ~Aug 14–16, and they vanish in minutes. · Sanlitun scam: never follow a friendly stranger or "art student" to a bar. · Peak crowds until Sep 1.
XI'AN 西安
DAYS 4–5 · AUG 24–25 · 2 NIGHTS
🏨 ~A$160–260🚇 L2L3L9
A two-night history-and-food stopover. Eat in the Muslim Quarter both nights. Save the big nights for later.
Top 3 Must-Dos
1. Terracotta Warriors. ¥120, timed entry. NOT on the metro — Line 9 to Huaqing Pool, then bus 602/613.
2. Muslim Quarter at night. Walk deep into the alleys; skip the stalls near the Drum Tower.
3. Cycle the City Wall at sunset. 14 km loop on top of a Ming-dynasty wall.
🎴 Gym & 🍸 Nightlife
Regidrago 雷吉铎拉戈 — SAGA Mall B2, Xiaozhai (L2 ∩ L3). Day 5, late AM — pair with the Big Wild Goose Pagoda.
Chill night. Defu Lane (德福巷) bar street. To meet people: hostel bars near the South Gate run pub crawls.
⚠ WATCH Terracotta Warriors: only a 7-day booking window — visit is Tue Aug 25, so it opens ~Aug 18. Real-name, passport required, sells out fast in summer peak. Official: bmy.com.cn · HSR Xi'an→Chengdu: 12306 opens ~Aug 11–12 (check both evenings).
CHENGDU 成都
DAYS 6–8 · AUG 26–28 · 3 NIGHTS
🏨 ~A$240–390🚇 L2L3L7L13
Spice capital, first big TCG haul, and the best underground techno in China. Day 7 is a big night.
Top 3 Must-Dos
1. Heming Teahouse, People's Park. Ear cleaning (采耳) with a tuning fork, ¥30–50. Morning, amid mahjong. THE Chengdu thing.
2. Kuanzhai Alley. Architecture + Sichuan street-food initiation.
3. Eastern Suburb Memory. Soviet electronics factory reborn as a cyber-retro art district.
🎴 Gym & 🍸 Nightlife
Spinda 晃晃斑 — Joy City LG, Wuhou (NOT Chunxi Rd). Day 7 PM + Dark World Zero to price-compare. Buy 3–4 boxes max — Shanghai is the bulk stop.
DAY 7 BIG NIGHT. Jah Bar / Beer Nest warm-up → Jiuyanqiao strip → T² (ex-.TAG, East Lake) or 339 (GAP/FLAT). Peaks 1–2am.
⚠ WATCH .TAG closed Aug 2025 → reopened as T² by East Lake Park. Axis and Clinic are gone; techno core moved to the 339 area. · Clubs check passports at the door and expect QR order-and-pay. · Jiuyanqiao is on the new Line 13, not Line 3 — old guides are wrong.
CHONGQING 重庆
DAYS 9–11 · AUG 29–31 · 3 NIGHTS
🏨 ~A$270–420🚇 L1L2 railL3L6L9
Cyberpunk 8D mountain city and hotpot ground zero. Vertically stacked — allow extra transfer time everywhere.
Top 3 Must-Dos
1. Liziba. The monorail that runs through an apartment block. Line 2 only.
2. Hongya Cave at night. Spirited Away made real. Shoot it, then leave to drink elsewhere.
3. Hotpot until midnight, then the lit-up riverside. This IS the Chongqing evening.
+ The Ring Garden City (TikTok find) — 20m steampunk-rainforest machine through 4 mall floors. Day 11 flex morning, architecture shot.
🎴 Gym & 🍸 Nightlife
⭐ OFFICIAL POKÉMON CENTRE — Raffles City Mall, Yuzhong (Chaotianmen, near the cableway). TikTok find, verified. Day 10 midday — walking distance from Jiefangbei. CQ just became a real TCG stop.
Fletchling 火箭雀 — Longfor Times Paradise Walk, Daping. Closest station Shiyoulu (L1). Day 10 PM, after Liziba. Promo count by end of Chongqing: 4 of 7.
Jiujie / 9th Street (Guanyinqiao, L3∩L9) — the real party district, peaks after 1am. Nut Livehouse + Deyi World to meet people. Bar 62 for the skyline.
⚠ WATCH Chongqing North station has TWO disconnected squares — ride L10 or the Loop if unsure. · Chongqing East is brand new (L6 branch, opened Jun 2025) — reconfirm the timetable. · Hongyadong and Nanbin Rd are tourist traps for drinking — 2–3× Jiujie prices. · Carry your physical passport (Space Club enforces it). · HSR out: 12306 opens ~Aug 17–18 (check both evenings).
ZHANGJIAJIE 张家界
DAYS 12–15 · SEP 1–4 · 4 NIGHTS
🏨 ~A$180–520🚌 NO METRO — buses · taxi · Didi
Avatar pillars and sea-of-clouds sunrises. The crowds vanish on Sep 1 — exactly when you arrive. Early to bed, every night.

1. Wulingyuan park at dawn. 5am starts. The pillar formations above the mist are the whole reason you came.

DAYS 16–21 · SEP 5–10 · 6 NIGHTS · LONGEST STAY
🏨 ~A$360–720🚇 L1L2L10Maglev
The TCG capital — two official gyms, the biggest haul of the trip — plus a 24-hour spa reset before the big night.
Top 3 Must-Dos
1. 1933 Old Millfun + 1000 Trees. Brutalist slaughterhouse turned Escher labyrinth, then the plant-covered mountain-building on Suzhou Creek (free — TikTok find). Same morning, 15 min apart.
2. The Bund at blue hour. 1920s banking halls against the Pudong supertalls.
3. The 24-hour spa (Day 20). Flat fee, unlimited pools, buffet, sleeping pods. Don't set an alarm.
🎴 Gym & 🍸 Nightlife
Cramorant 古月鸟 (Longemont 3F, Zhongshan Park) + Cetoddle 轻身鳕 (Wujiaochang Wanda, L10/L18). Both Day 18, then Baimi Xiangxie to price-compare. Bulk-buy city.

🍜 Li Baixie 李百蟹 (TikTok find) — crab roe noodles, reserve ahead + request the Bund-window seat. Day 18 dinner. · Anfu Rd + Wukang Mansion for the FFC wander. · MONA @Fotografiska photography museum option.

HONG KONG 香港
DAYS 22–25 · SEP 11–14 · 4 NIGHTS · EXIT POINT
🏨 ~A$560–680🚇 Tsuen WanIslandAirport Exp
Outside the Firewall — Google and WhatsApp just work. The only stop where Japanese, English AND Chinese cards sit side by side, and the easiest city to meet people.
Top 3 Must-Dos
1. OZONE, 118F ICC (Day 24). World's highest bar. One drink — the view is the point. Smart dress.
2. Kowloon Walled City Park. Stand where 33,000 people lived on 2.6 hectares. The cyberpunk pilgrimage.
3. Ding Ding tram, front seat, top deck, after dark. HK$3.30. The anti-OZONE, and just as good.
🎴 Cards & 🍸 Nightlife
No official gym — but In's Point, Sino Centre + HobbyX on Day 22; Moonroad + K11 Pokémon Hub on Day 25. Traditional Chinese product, not Simplified.
DAY 23 BIG NIGHT. The Holywell's (140 Hollywood Rd) → SoHo (Quinary, Old Man) → LKF → Cassio. Wan Chai (Carnegie's, The Wanch) for the grit.
